Their are so many things that aggravate me about social media, here In talk about some of them, I will try to talk about things that haven’t discussed to death (the polarizing effect of social media, cyber-bullying etc) but other much more minor annoyances 1.

not being able to mass import someones feed (Twitter)

I want to find good links to read and stuff, the closest I can think of is flip-feed, however their doesn’t appear to be a Firefox extension and it doesn’t seem to allow you to mass-follow, it should be available by default for Twitter not some obscure extension. If there so worried about echo-chamber.

This is easy with RSS, just look for the option of importing and exporting OPML file.

the brokenness of the UI (Twitter)

This is better described visually, see grumpy.website for examples. It is such a garbage experience.

the fake wall

Why can’t I view Instagram without an account? It is so stupid, their is no technical reason to do that. It is just pure market grift.

getting the benefits without the drawbacks

Their are benefits to social media don’t get me wrong. So many good accounts on are there, interesting people and loads more. You have to be picky and get out with the bullshit.

I think most useful social media is twitter (use nitter) and instagram (use bibliogram) both I believe you can follow on RSS, I also recommend to use teddit (reddit viewer) on the phone.
