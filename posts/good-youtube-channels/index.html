Do you need Spotify? (good YouTube channels)

2021/05/28

  • Ambiance has a lot of great electronic albums (mostly 1990s).
  • Saturn Archives post a lot of avant garde pieces that are interesting.
  • Jimmy Eden Moon mainly 90s hiphop.
  • Kuma’s campfire Good catalog of Japanese albums a lot pretty avant garde.
  • Xerf Xpec Wonderful collection of Japanese Jazz Fusion.
  • Terminal Passage Mainly psychedelic and Prog but very ecletic.
  • Peter Niktos Mainly 60s and 70s psydelic.
  • Elshay Yehezkel Awesome Italian music.
  • Nicolás Guzmán Ecletic and extremly wonderful.
  • Ricardo Maraña Mainly easy listening.
  • off1k Amiga tracker music
  • Niko’s 8BitStereo All NES music, wonderful if you love chiptunes.
  • rarest records obscure, usually 60s and 70s album.
  • MartyMcflies Old Japanese image albums.

The only problem is that it is kind of difficult to download and channels can be deleted (this will be expanded apon in a future article). Still you can live without Spotify.
