how do paper journaling / logging

2021/05/15

I feel like this is topic that is very misunderstood, probably because most people haven’t maintained one. I journaled for more than a year and have made 9 journals. Here are some tips I recommend.

  • write in print (not cursive): legibility becomes a serious issue with written writing. 99% of the pictures posted on the internet in order for people for decipher what it means is cursive (see r/cursive for many examples there are other places). I personally can’t read much of my cursive while I can easily read my print.
  • think of it as a blog, do not make it a topic book: blogs come from written journals! Journals are append only, I have tried using a binder as well but it is not very comfortable. You will get the most enjoyment if you consider it a log.
  • tag your pages: see this article, it makes it very easy to find things.
  • always write the date: You want to know when you wrote things.
  • write in pen: I find it easier, I use to pencil but it is kinda cumbersome to write a lot. Most writers use to only use pen for a reason I think.
  • use the benefits of the medium: have margin notes, margin drawings, drawings, diagrams, multiple colors, interesting visuals etc.
