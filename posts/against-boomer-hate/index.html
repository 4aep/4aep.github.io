One of the biggest and most vile critiques I see on the internet and in many intellectual circles is against boomers. This essay I will present reasons why I consider it to be dumb.

Disclaimer: I am not a boomer and actually don’t know many boomers, I think this makes more impartial? I do think their are valid social issues but boomers are not a cancer plaguing the world.

the concept of “social generations” (Boomer, X, Y, Z)

Most sociologist don’t believe in it and don’t use it as a valid category in their work, their is a reason for that.

The sociologist Phil Cohen is prominent spokesperson against social generations, his reasons against it

  • irregular categories without justification (people can’t sort themselves)
  • not actual generations (15 year intervals is not a actual generation), really cohorts
  • marketing names promote stereotyping (judging unrepresentativly) and clickbait confirmation bias
  • sacrifice info with potential benefit: social scientist use 10 or X year interval cohorts or context-specific (kids who grew up after 911 or young adults who faced the COVID lock-downs). Why use such arbitrary standards that many times reveal false patterns due to the irregular ages.

On the Pew survey he talked about, asking people what generations they are apart of and people can’t sort themselves out. The only people who could kinda do it are Boomers, since it was really a demographic jump, but I haven’t seen an anti-boomerist explain to me how population jumps somehow lead to strange and unique social-psychological that lead them to extreme greed, libertine-thinking, debauchery or xenophobia. Also the end-point is total arbitrary as the event kept on going after 64.

This means I am also against Xer hate, Z hate (now their usually called “zoomers”) or “gen alpha” hate, It’s all fake.

the conservative critique of boomers

Strange as it may be but their are people who hate boomers for literally polar opposite reasons, conservatives see them as pioneers of debauchery and selfishness while progressives see them as pinnacles of avarice and bigotry. I guess the greed aspect of Boomers they share but empirically Boomers are less narcissistic (as measured by NPI diagnostic) then younger “generations” 1.
