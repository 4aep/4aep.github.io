- [Ambiance](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCAw0NyC_6y-se4zcQGbEgHA/videos)
  has a lot of great electronic albums (mostly 1990s).
- [Saturn Archives](https://www.youtube.com/c/SaturnArchives/videos) post a lot
  of avant garde pieces that are interesting.
- [Jimmy Eden Moon](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCQDsxiuL3i6gX4M9xDTT51w)
  mainly 90s hiphop.
- [Kuma's campfire](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC-jln7H9BVxgX1zZvDcDatg/videos) Good catalog of Japanese albums a lot pretty avant garde.
- [Xerf Xpec](https://www.youtube.com/c/XerfXpec/videos) Wonderful collection of Japanese Jazz Fusion.
- [Terminal Passage](https://www.youtube.com/c/TerminalPassage/) Mainly psychedelic and Prog but very ecletic.
- [Peter Niktos](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCJIs6na9YvxvhTKNP5rX9DA/videos) Mainly 60s and 70s psydelic.
- [Elshay Yehezkel](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCMa5tZ6supZ40A0gH1ysCrg/videos) Awesome Italian music.
- [Nicolás Guzmán](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC10ffUrAubaEi2iwDRsmdbg/videos) Ecletic and extremly wonderful.
- [Ricardo Maraña](https://www.youtube.com/c/RicardoMara%C3%B1a/videos) Mainly easy listening.
- [off1k](https://www.youtube.com/user/off1k/videos) Amiga tracker music
- [Niko's 8BitStereo](https://www.youtube.com/c/Wiiguy309/videos) All NES music, wonderful if you love chiptunes.

The only problem is that it is kinda difficult to download and channels can be deleted. Still you can live without Spotify.
